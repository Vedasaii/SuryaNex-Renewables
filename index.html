Government Support

Direct Subsidies from PM Surya Ghar Yojana

The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) makes solar installation more affordable than ever. Homeowners receive structured Central Financial Assistance (CFA) directly into their linked bank accounts within 30 days of project synchronization.

System Capacity (kW) Subsidy Amount (Rs.) Key Highlights
1 kW System ₹30,000 Best for small homes (approx 100 units/mo)
2 kW System ₹60,000 Ideal for double-story houses (200 units/mo)
3 kW & Above ₹78,000 (Maximum) Optimal capacity for ACs and heavy appliances

* Note: Direct subsidy is applicable to residential rooftops only. Net metering is managed via APEPDCL guidelines.

Subsidy Compliance Notice: To receive the central CFA, installations must use Domestic Content Requirement (DCR) solar panels manufactured by brands listed in the MNRE's Approved List of Models & Manufacturers (ALMM), connected via standard bi-directional net meters.

How We Work

Your Simple 5-Step Journey to Solar

We make transitioning to clean energy seamless, managing all regulatory approvals and technical deployments.

1

Site Assessment & Solar Audit

Our engineers perform digital shadow analyses and check electrical grid conditions to propose the optimal capacity for your property in Rajahmundry.

2

Custom Engineering & System Design

We model customized structural foundations and layout designs, calculating optimal angles to maximize daily solar irradiance and secure high wind resilience.

3

APEPDCL Approvals & Net-Metering

We process all permissions and liaison with the Andhra Pradesh Eastern Power Distribution Company (APEPDCL) to prepare for your bidirectional solar net meter integration.

4

High-Fidelity Installation & Safety Checks

Our trained specialists complete the physical deployment of structural channels, solar modules, heavy-gauge copper wiring, grounding kits, and fire safety systems.

5

Grid Synchronization & Subsidy Release

Once certified by the discom inspector, the solar plant synchronizes with the power grid. We submit all documentation to deposit your subsidy directly into your bank.
